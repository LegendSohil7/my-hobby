MY FAVOURITE SPORT

Football is my hobby. I mostly play it in my free time and I find it a competitive, passionate sport which I love. I am in Year 7 and find that football keeps me healthy, fit and it is a good way to exercise. It is also a chance to meet others and show your inner self!

Review On The Movies

Avengers Endgame
Excellent film to culminate MCU ride

I watched it today with my kids and they loved it. You should have a minimal understanding of the previous story, or you will miss some of the fun. This film is the pure definition of an emotional roller coaster and throughout it's run time brings about fascination, humor, sadness, incredible excitement, and sheer finality. This is not your typical superhero movie and some viewers used to the entertaining, nonstop action style of the previous films may be disappointed, but I think that this movie brilliantly satisfies and subverts expectations.
